# 📊 Tableau Dashboards Portfolio

A collection of interactive Tableau dashboards built on two real-world datasets: the **Superstore Sales** dataset and the **Netflix Titles** dataset. Each dashboard explores a distinct analytical angle, from financial performance and product trends to content strategy and audience ratings.

---

## 📁 Dashboards

### 1. 🏦 Superstore Financial Overview
**[View on Tableau Public →](https://public.tableau.com/shared/TJWM938KB?:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)**

A high-level financial summary of the Superstore business, focusing on profitability and revenue performance across regions and customer segments.

**Key questions answered:**
- Which regions and segments drive the most revenue and profit?
- Where are margins being compressed?
- How does overall financial health break down across the business?

**Tools & techniques:** KPIs, geographic maps, segment-level profit analysis, filter actions

---

### 2. 📈 Product Sales Trends
**[View on Tableau Public →](https://public.tableau.com/views/ProductSalesDashboard_17772575467620/ProductSalesDashboard)**

A time-series focused dashboard tracking how sales evolve across product lines, surfacing seasonality and growth trends at the category and sub-category level.

**Key questions answered:**
- How have sales trended over time by product category?
- Are there seasonal patterns in purchasing behavior?
- Which sub-categories are growing or declining?

**Tools & techniques:** Time-series line charts, trend lines, category-level drill-down, date filtering

---

### 3. 🛍️ Product & Category Sales Overview
**[View on Tableau Public →](https://public.tableau.com/views/SuperstoreSalesData_17773604569910/Dashboard1)**

A comprehensive cross-sectional view of sales and profitability broken down by product category and sub-category, designed to identify top performers and underperformers.

**Key questions answered:**
- Which categories and sub-categories generate the most sales and profit?
- What is the relationship between discount rates and profitability?
- Which products are underperforming relative to their sales volume?

**Tools & techniques:** Bar charts, scatter plots, profitability matrices, category filters

---

### 4. 🎬 Netflix Content & Ratings Overview
**[View on Tableau Public →** *(link coming soon)*]

An exploratory analysis of Netflix's content library, examining the distribution of titles by type, rating, genre, and release year to understand how the platform's catalog has evolved.

**Key questions answered:**
- How is Netflix's content split between Movies and TV Shows?
- What content ratings dominate the library?
- How has the volume and composition of new content changed over time?

**Tools & techniques:** Stacked bar charts, tree maps, rating distribution, time-based content analysis
